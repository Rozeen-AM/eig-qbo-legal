Privacy Policy - EIG QBO Integration

EIG Exhibitions Interiors LLC - Dubai, UAE - Effective 20 July 2026

1. What this application is

The "EIG QBO Integration" is a private, internal software integration used solely by EIG Exhibitions Interiors LLC to read from and post bookkeeping corrections to its own QuickBooks Online company. It is not offered to, installed by, or made available to any third party, and is not listed on any public app marketplace.

2. Data we access

Through Intuit's QuickBooks Online API, the integration accesses the company's own accounting data (accounts, transactions, journal entries, company profile) strictly for internal reconciliation and bookkeeping. It accesses only the QuickBooks company owned by EIG Exhibitions Interiors LLC.

3. How data is used

Data is used only for the company's internal accounting operations (reconciling bank accounts and correcting bookkeeping entries). It is not sold, rented, or shared with any third party, and is not used for advertising or profiling.

4. Storage and security

Access credentials are stored locally on the company's own device, outside any shared cloud folder, and are not transmitted to any third party other than Intuit as required to operate the QuickBooks Online API. No customer or personal data is copied to external systems.

5. Data retention

The integration does not maintain a separate copy of accounting data; it reads and writes directly to QuickBooks Online. Local operational logs are retained only for audit and are kept on the company's own device.
